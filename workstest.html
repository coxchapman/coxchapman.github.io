Work

Opower

Technical Project Manager • March 2014 -Present

Opower combines a cloud-based platform, big data, and behavioral science to help utilities around the world reduce energy consumption and improve their relationship with their customers. I lead a team of project managers and engineers to deliver Opower's technology to our utility clients.

Associate Technical Project Manager• February 2013 - March 2014

Consumer Marketing and Operations Specialist• May 2012 - February 2013

Arc Finance

Project Coordinator • November 2010 - May 2012

The mission of Arc Finance is to promote and expand access to financing for energy, water and other basic needs to build the income and assets of poor people around the world. At Arc, I managed renewable-energy finance projects around the world, from the Philippines to Haiti.

Stack+Co

Assistant Project Manager • October 2009 - November 2010

Stack+Co is a design-build firm based in Providence, Rhode Island. At Stack I coordinated the project management of a number of construction projects, including the Box Office and a container clinic for Care 2 Communities which was later deployed to Haiti.

Spirit of South Carolina

Educator and Deckhand •June 2009 - October 2009

The Spirit of South Carolina is a traditional wooden tall ship that offered experiential learning programs to high school and college students up and down the East Coast. As part of a professional crew of nine, I taught seamanship, leadership, and environmental awareness.

Maine to Nova Scotia

In the summer of 2015 I sailed from Maine to Nova Scotia. The 180-nm trip took us about 36 hours at 6kts. To plan, I learned how to read GRIB files and prepared an optimized route based on forecasted weather data. For a short video of the trip, click the link below

Great American Road Trip

In the summer of 2015 I drove 8500 miles around America. We went from New England to British Columbia and back, in 36 days. We only stayed in hotels two nights; the rest was with friends or on the ground. We saw land art in the Nevada desert, hiked in Glacier National Park, and drove Detroit's Grand Avenue.
